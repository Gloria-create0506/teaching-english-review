Find Evidence · 原文定位

答案不靠猜，回原文找证据

A few more monkeys and apes gathered around Sun Wukong and the gibbon. They looked scared. "A demon attacked us," said the gibbon. "We tried to fight him, but he was too powerful. He stole most of our things and kidnapped many of us."

Wukong was furious. "Who is he? Where does he live?" "He is called the Demon of Chaos," said the gibbon. "He came from the north." "Wait here," Wukong ordered. "I will handle the demon."

He leaped into the air and flew north. Wukong soon spotted a gloomy mountain. Dark clouds hung over it. Wukong landed on the mountain and found a cave. Two guards stood at the entrance, practicing sword fighting.

"I am king of the Fruit and Flower Mountain!" yelled Wukong. "The Demon of Chaos attacked my subjects! I want revenge!" The guards shrieked and ran into the cave.

A moment later the Demon of Chaos walked outside. He looked at Wukong and laughed. "You are just a little monkey!" Wukong jumped out of the way and then flew at the demon. Wukong hit the demon on the nose.

Wukong pulled out a handful of his own hair and blew on it. Each strand of hair turned into a fake monkey, exactly like Wukong. All at once the fake monkeys attacked the demon, punching and kicking him. "It is a magic spell," said Wukong. "It is called Body Beyond the Body."

The Demon of Chaos was soon destroyed. Wukong chased away the guards and went into the cave. He found all the monkeys and apes that the demon had kidnapped. "I will take you home!" Wukong smiled.

The next morning Wukong woke up early. "Other demons might attack us," he thought. "We must be ready to protect ourselves." Wukong spent the day teaching his subjects how to fight. Before long, the monkeys and apes were a fierce army.
